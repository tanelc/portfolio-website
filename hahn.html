Hahn

About
Hahn awareness campaign for tab water. Germanys tab water is of highest quality, low in cost, accessable in every household, and better for the environment. The campaign pretends to be another fancy new sparkling water brand and then reveals to be tab water from the local water works.
Three freelancers teamed up to bring this campaign to life. I focused on strategy, creating the brand and artwork.

Tools

Phase 1: Introduction and Awareness.Billboards with funky headlines reveal more information about this new brand, bit by bit.


Phase 2: "Hahn" is presented as a fancy sparkling water brand with minimal design and funky headlines.

Phase 3: The final billboards show that "Hahn" is a fictional sparkling water brand from the local water works in Hamburg.
