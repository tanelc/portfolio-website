Logodesign

Devil horns engage with a soup bowl - a clear and simple logo for Devil Soups, using black backgrounds wherever possible to add some drama. This small business applies its logo on huge variety of different surfaces, sizes and materials. This calls for a reduced amount of colours and several different versions of the logo to fit every need.

The Fleet

What's a fresh logo without a newly designed foodtruck? This charming old Metro-truck is the first to receive the new branding and goes by the name "Baby".

Packaging Design

Canned soups are sold in company shops such as Airbus, directly from the truck or via website. To launch the project three soups sorts and two different jar sizes are offered. From meaty Mexican stews to vegan Asian inspired carrot soups. For one or two hungry eaters.
